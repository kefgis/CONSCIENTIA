What We Do

At CONSCIENTIA, we provide:

  • Research in forestry, NRM, climate change, agriculture, and agroforestry
  • Training: performance-enhancing short-term and tailored programs
  • Consultancy: project design, monitoring & evaluation, workshops, and technical advisory services

    • Our Values

    Our core value is partnership. We strive to be a reliable ally in efforts that make a difference in people’s lives.

  • Integrity, punctuality, and professional excellence guide our work.
  • We tailor our services to meet client needs with relevance and effectiveness.
  • Continuous feedback ensures that our solutions remain practical and impactful.

    • Our Aim

    CONSCIENTIA exists to empower organizations, communities, and individuals by combining knowledge, training, and consultancy to address pressing challenges in natural resources and climate change.
